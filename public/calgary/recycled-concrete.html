Recycled Concrete in Calgary — Crushed Concrete Aggregate for Base and Fill

Recycled crushed concrete — concrete from demolition and construction, processed and crushed to spec — is one of the cheapest and most environmentally responsible aggregates in Calgary. It performs comparably to virgin road crush for most non-engineered applications, at a meaningful cost saving.

What recycled concrete is

Recycled crushed concrete is concrete and rebar-free concrete waste run through a crusher and screener to produce a graded aggregate. Calgary recyclers process it to specs comparable to virgin road crush (20mm minus) and pit run.

Common Calgary uses

  • Driveway base — direct substitute for virgin road crush.
  • Parking lot base
  • Trench backfill
  • Structural fill (with engineering review)
  • Erosion control / rip-rap (larger fragment sizes)

Recycled concrete vs. virgin aggregate

  • Cost: recycled is typically lower
  • Performance: equivalent for most non-engineered applications
  • Environmental: diverts material from landfill; lower embodied carbon
  • Limitations: not always specified for engineered structural fill

Frequently asked questions

Is recycled concrete as good as virgin road crush?
For most non-engineered driveway, parking, and base applications, yes. For engineered structural fill, verify the spec with your engineer.
Does recycled concrete compact like road crush?
Yes — the fines and angular fragments compact well.
Can I use it under pavers?
Yes — common alternative to virgin crush for paver bases.
