Calgary Dirt Disposal Cost Calculator — Tipping Fees vs. Dirtlink

Calgary's clean-fill tipping rate is genuinely cheap — $0 to $10 per tonne — but that's almost never the full cost of disposing of a load. Trucking hours, scale-house waits, the City's commercial clean-fill approval process, and the very real risk of being billed at the basic sanitary rate ($113/tonne) or commercial surcharge rate ($180/tonne) all stack on top. This calculator estimates the real all-in cost of disposing your load at Shepard, East Calgary, Spyhill, or West Calgary landfills — and shows what it would cost to route the same material through Dirtlink instead.

What the calculator shows

The result varies dramatically with what's actually in your load:

  • Pure clean fill — tipping is cheap ($0–$10/tonne), but trucking time, scale-house waits, and the City's commercial clean-fill approval process still cost you. Dirtlink savings here come from time saved, not tipping fees.
  • Sod, mixed, or contaminated material — billed at $113/tonne (basic sanitary) or up to $180/tonne (commercial surcharge with recyclables). For a tandem load (~18 tonnes), that's $2,000+ in tipping alone before you factor trucking. Dirtlink savings here are dollars, not just hours.
  • Small loads (under 250 kg) — $25 flat rate at the landfill. Almost always more than your hauling cost to a Dirtlink match.

Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the calculator?
Estimates are based on typical Calgary tipping rates and trucking norms. For precise project costs, use your actual scale-house receipts and trucking rates.
What's the average Calgary tipping fee for clean fill?
Clean fill is $0–$10/tonne — easily the cheapest category at Calgary landfills. Mixed loads and sod jump to $113/tonne; commercial surcharge tier is $180/tonne. Calculator pre-fills current rates; verify the latest with the City of Calgary if a project is on the line.
Does Dirtlink charge a fee?
Listings are free. Dirtlink doesn't take a cut of the transaction.
What if I can't find a taker fast enough?
Most clean-fill listings in Calgary match within hours during peak season. For tight timelines, the landfill remains a fallback.
Can I use this calculator for surrounding markets?
Yes — Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, and Chestermere all draw on the same Calgary landfill network. Adjust trucking distance accordingly.
