Popular landscape rock types in Calgary

  • Rundle rock — locally quarried, warm grey-brown, the default for naturalistic Calgary landscaping.
  • Limestone — pale grey, clean look, common in modern designs.
  • River rock — smooth, rounded, in sizes from 1" to softball-sized. See the dedicated River Rock page.
  • Granite / decorative crushed — crushed angular stone in colours from white to black.
  • Lava rock — porous red/black rock, lighter weight, used for accent and mulch alternative.
  • Pea gravel — small rounded stone for paths and ground cover.

How much landscape rock do you need?

At a typical 2–3" depth: 1 cubic yard covers ~80–130 sq ft. A standard 8'x10' bed at 3" depth needs about 1 cubic yard. Larger front yards typically need 3–8 cubic yards.

Pickup vs. delivery

Most Calgary suppliers offer both. Pickup is cheaper for small quantities (under 1 yard); delivery makes sense for full or partial loads. Some Dirtlink listings include spreading by a paired contractor.

Frequently asked questions

What's the most popular landscape rock in Calgary?
Rundle rock — locally quarried, warm tones, naturalistic. Limestone is the second most common.
How deep should landscape rock be?
2–3" minimum for visual coverage and weed suppression. Deeper for high-traffic areas.
Do I need landscape fabric under rock?
Almost always yes — without it, rock sinks into the soil and weeds grow up through.
Can I have landscape rock spread for me?
Some Dirtlink listings offer paired spreading; others are delivery-only. Filter by "spreading available."
