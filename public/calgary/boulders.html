Boulder types in Calgary

  • Rundle boulders — locally quarried, warm grey-brown.
  • Granite boulders — harder, varied colours.
  • Limestone boulders — pale grey, clean modern look.
  • Rip rap / armor stone — spec-graded large stone for erosion control.

Common uses

  • Feature stones — single accent boulders in front yards.
  • Boulder retaining walls — natural alternative to engineered walls.
  • Dry creek beds — paired with smaller river rock.
  • Rip rap — slopes, drainage channels, shoreline.

Buying boulders in Calgary

Most boulder suppliers sell by the tonne for rip-rap and by the piece for feature stones. Loading and placement require equipment — confirm whether the supplier delivers and places, or delivery only.

Frequently asked questions

How are boulders priced in Calgary?
By the tonne for bulk rip-rap and armor stone; by the piece for feature stones (priced on size and type).
Can I get boulders placed?
Some suppliers offer placement; others are delivery-only. Filter listings by "placement available."
What size boulder for a feature stone?
Most front-yard features use stones 18"–36" in diameter. Larger statement stones run 36"+.
Do I need a permit to install large boulders?
For homeowner front-yard placement, generally no. For retaining walls over 1m or boulders near property lines, check Calgary bylaws.
How much does a feature boulder cost in Calgary?
Highly variable by size and type. Live listings show current pricing.
