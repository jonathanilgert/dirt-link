Simple Process

How DirtLink Works

Three steps to connect your site with the right material match nearby.

1

Drop a Pin

Mark your site on the map. Tell us what you have or what you need — material type, quantity, and access conditions.

2

Browse Nearby Sites

See all active material sites in your area on the live map. Filter by material type, tested status, and pin type.

3

Connect Directly

Message the site contact directly through DirtLink. No brokers, no middlemen — just two companies solving a problem together.

Built For

Who Uses DirtLink?

Built for contractors, excavators, developers, and site managers across Canada.

Have Material

You Have Excess Fill

Your excavation generated more material than you expected. Instead of paying to haul it to a landfill, find a nearby site that needs exactly what you have.

  • Excavation contractors
  • General contractors
  • Developers clearing land
  • Municipal projects
Need Material

You Need Fill or Aggregate

Your project needs fill, aggregate, or organic material. Find a local source and coordinate delivery directly — no supplier markups.

  • Landscaping companies
  • Road & infrastructure crews
  • Foundation & grading contractors
  • Property developers
