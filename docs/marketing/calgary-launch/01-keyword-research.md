# Calgary SEO Keyword Research — Dirtlink

A comprehensive keyword set for Calgary, AB. Organized by search intent and material type so it maps directly to the page structure in `02-page-content.md`.

> **Important:** Volumes below are estimates based on general industry knowledge. Validate exact volumes in Google Keyword Planner (free with any Google Ads account) before prioritizing. Volumes shift seasonally — Calgary's spring (April-June) and fall (Sept-Oct) see major spikes for almost every term in this list.

---

## Calgary-specific context worth weaving into pages

**Local landfills (drives "where to dump dirt" intent):**
- Shepard Landfill (108 St SE) — large municipal facility
- East Calgary Landfill (75 St SE)
- Spyhill Landfill (NW)
- West Calgary Landfill

**Active growth corridors (high construction volume):**
- NE: Cornerstone, Livingston, Carrington, Glacier Ridge, Redstone, Skyview Ranch
- SE: Mahogany, Auburn Bay, Seton, Walden, Legacy, Rangeview
- NW: Trinity Hills, Greenwich, Symons Valley, Sherwood, Nolan Hill
- S: Belmont, Pine Creek, Yorkville
- Surrounding markets to mention as related: Airdrie, Cochrane, Okotoks, Chestermere, Strathmore, High River, Olds

**Calgary-specific terminology to use natively:**
- "Gumbo" — local term for sticky clay soil
- "Pit run" — preferred over "bank run gravel"
- "Road crush" — preferred over "crusher run"
- "20mm minus" or "1" minus" — common spec language
- "Screened topsoil" — quality differentiator buyers search for

**Calendar drivers:**
- Spring road bans (typically late March – early June) restrict heavy hauling and create urgency
- Stampede prep (June) drives temporary surface work
- Fall yard cleanup (Sept-Oct) drives homeowner topsoil/compost searches
- Winter (Dec-Feb) is low season — focus content on planning-stage searches
